About Us

We're an independent company formed by 5 students, from the renowned Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas, who made a web application that allows all medical professionals to have easy access to these legal resources, in the form of medical lawyers.

Our Services

For lawyers:

Be able to work with us to help with the various features we offer in our application.

For doctors:

Receive legal support from some of our lawyers, most of whom specialized in medicine-related cases.

For everyone:

With our suscription, you'll receive tips and advice about the legal aspects of medicine, with the help of our lawyers.

Our Plans

Legal Support

For doctors who are looking for specialized legal assistance from our lawyers, who are experts in the legal aspects of medicine.
